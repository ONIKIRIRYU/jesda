Spider-man no way home

Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in the Silver Age of Comic Books

sonic the hedgehog 2

After settling in Green Hills, Sonic is eager to prove that he has what it takes to be a true hero. His test comes when Dr. Robotnik returns with a new partner, Knuckles, in search of a mystical emerald that has the power to destroy civilizations. Sonic teams up with his own sidekick, Tails, and together they embark on a globe-trotting journey to find the emerald before it falls into the wrong hands.

fantastic beasts the secrets of dumbledore

Dumbledore assembles a team of wizards, witches and a Muggle baker to oppose the rise of Gellert Grindelwald. They hatch a plan to confuse Grindelwald and stop him from attaining political power.
